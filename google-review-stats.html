20 Google Review Statistics You Must Know in 2025

Online reviews have a massive impact on how customers perceive and interact with businesses. Understanding the power of Google Reviews is essential for growth in today's digital marketplace. Here are some of the most important statistics to keep in mind.

Why Google Reviews Matter

  • 88% of customers read Google reviews to determine the quality of a local business before engaging with them.
  • About 74% of consumers feel that positive Google reviews help them trust a business more.
  • 57.5% of all online reviews are on Google, making it the most dominant review platform.
  • Top-ranking businesses on Google have an average of 47 reviews.

The Psychology Behind Reviews

Google reviews create powerful social proof. People tend to imitate the actions of others when they are unsure of a decision. Positive reviews make prospective customers view a business as more believable and dependable.

  • 87% of customers say they engage with businesses that have an average star rating of 3-4 stars on Google.
  • 89% of customers prefer to check out online reviews before making purchase decisions.

Trends & Best Practices

Staying current with review trends is crucial. The recency of a review is a major factor for consumers, and how a business responds to feedback can be just as important as the review itself.

  • 73% of consumers only trust reviews written in the last month.
  • 81% of consumers are more likely to check Google reviews before visiting a business.
  • An incredible 44.6% of customers will still engage with your business if you respond to negative reviews.
